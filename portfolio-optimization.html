Project Introduction

This innovative project combines modern portfolio theory with artificial intelligence to create a next-generation investment optimization framework. By leveraging advanced machine learning techniques and traditional financial models, we deliver more intelligent and data-driven investment decisions.

AI-Powered Analysis

Utilizes advanced language models and machine learning algorithms to analyze market trends and generate insights.

Hybrid Approach

Combines traditional financial models (MPT, Black-Litterman) with modern AI techniques for enhanced decision-making.

Real-Time Processing

Processes market data in real-time to provide up-to-date portfolio recommendations and risk assessments.

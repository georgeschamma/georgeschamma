What is RAG Framework?

The Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) framework is a powerful AI architecture that combines the knowledge retrieval capabilities of large language models with custom data sources. This innovative approach enables AI systems to provide more accurate, contextual, and up-to-date responses by referencing specific information from your data while maintaining the natural language understanding of foundation models.

Key Benefits

  • Enhanced Accuracy: Leverages both AI language models and your specific data
  • Real-time Updates: Incorporates the latest information from your knowledge base
  • Domain Expertise: Specializes in your field through custom data integration
  • Reduced Hallucination: Grounds responses in factual, retrievable information
